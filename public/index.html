IGUANAS

An Amazing Animal

Description

The iguana is a reptile belonging to the Iguana family. Its size ranges from 15 cm to 2 meters in length and can weigh around 15 kg. When young, they have an all-green coloration, and as they mature, a grayish color predominates with crests or spines on their back.

The iguana's diet varies throughout its life stages, but most of its years are herbivorous. They inhabit tropical areas with abundant vegetation and can be domesticated animals.
